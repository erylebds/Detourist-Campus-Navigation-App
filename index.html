About

Detourist is a campus navigation system designed to help students locate rooms, view campus layouts, and navigate safely during emergencies. It provides real-time alerts, interactive maps, and emergency routing options managed by administrators.

Our mission is to enhance campus safety and accessibility through innovative technology, ensuring that students can navigate their campus with confidence and ease.

Join us in making campus navigation safer and more efficient for everyone!
